How was the vote spread among counties in the Alabama 2017 Special Election?

To answer this I had to scrape a table from CNN and convert it to a CSV. As opposed to getting each counties results one by one from the Alabama state department. I then added a column to the table to give a % margin of victory in order to shade each county. Lastly, I merged this with US Census county shapes which I was able to merge in Google Fusion and filter down to Alabama counties.
